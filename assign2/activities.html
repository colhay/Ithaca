Club Soccer The Ithaca College Men's Club Soccer team competes in a fall league against schools in the central New York area in an effort to qualify for a regional and national tournament. In the winter the club team plays in an indoor league at a soccer facility in Lansing, NY. In the spring the club team schedules games against other schools in the central New York area.
